Test Information

This test takes on average 15-20 minutes and is completely made up of 25 multiple choice questions.

The California Written Driving Test is available to anyone who is over the age of 15 1/2 years old.

Scoring:
  • How many questions will be on this practice test?

    25

  • How many questions can you miss and still pass?

    5

  • Passing score

    80%

Please take your time when answering the questions. This test isn't timed!
